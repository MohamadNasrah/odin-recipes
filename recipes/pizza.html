Pizza

Description

Pizza, the yummiest food on the planet dare i say.
today i'm going to teach you how to create this heavenly pie, which is an easy process to do.

But first, lets check the ingredients.

Ingerdients

  • FLour, 500 grams
  • Yeast
  • Tomatoes, four pieces
  • Garlic, two gloves
  • Pepper, two pieces
  • Onions, three pieces
  • Olive Oil
  • Canned Mushroom, one can
  • Basil leaves
  • Cheese, preferably Mozarella

Steps

  1. First, you need to mix the flour and yeast with some olive oil and salt to taste.
  2. then mix them untill you create an elastic dough, cover it and wait for it to double in size
  3. while the dough is resting, chop the onions and crush the tomatoes
  4. saute the onions and tomatoes and finally add minced garlic and salt to taste
  5. when the sauce cools, blend it in a food procesor
  6. knead the dough for a little bit then roll it to the desired pie size
  7. add the sauce on top of it then the cheese in small pieces to cover the pie
  8. put the pizza in the oven at 250c for 15 mintes then add the basil leaves ontop of the melted cheese
