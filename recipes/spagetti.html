Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a favored meal by people of all age, and today i'll show you how to create a simple and basic dish.

Ingerdients

  • Spaghetti, 500 grams
  • Tomatoes
  • Ground Beef, 300 grams
  • Onions, four pieces
  • Tomatoe Paste, two TBSs

Steps

  1. Boil the Spaghetti in salted water then drain it
  2. chop the onions and crush the tomatoes
  3. saute the onions first then add the beef
  4. when the beef is cooked add the tomatoe paste and stir
  5. add the crushed tomatoes and salt to taste
  6. when everything is cooked drop the spaghetti
  7. mix everything inside the pot until it is mixed evenly
